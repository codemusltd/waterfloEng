history

Waterflo Engineering was initially founded in the late 60s as Lacoste Irrigation. It was mainly manufacturing aluminum irrigation fittings. In the early 80s the company was bought by the Cornish family who renamed it Waterflo Irrigation. They expanded the activities to manufacturing brass sprinklers design and installation of mainly pressurized turnkey irrigation projects. Mainly for irrigating wheat. In 2000 the company was bought as a going concern by the current owners. The companyâ€™s purpose is to design, construct and commission sustainable water supply and Irrigation projects. Most of the projects are turnkey although some times we do construction only or design only. In 2013 Waterflo was registered in Mozambique and has been doing mainly construction of irrigation projects.
